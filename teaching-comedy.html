Comedy

2027

There's a stage. Comedians do their sets. I'm in the audience, and after each one I give feedback — live, in the room, while everyone is still there.

It started as an experiment. What if you treated comedy like a craft you could actually talk about, mid-performance, with the person who just did it? The comedians were nervous about it. Then they weren't. The feedback loop got tight enough that you could feel people improving in real time.
