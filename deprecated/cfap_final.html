Frequently asked
Is this a cult?
No. Not yet, anyways.
Is the slapping game real?
Yes. You slap each other with increasing force until one of you gets triggered. This is the equanimity module. The slapping is not the point. The point is noticing what happens right before the slapping becomes a problem.
What is "post-rationality"?
Rationality was a container. Post-rationality is what happens when you've internalized it so hard you no longer need to carry it visibly. We think it needs the body. Hence the cold showers.
Is this organized?
It is a cabinet of curiosities. There is no principle from which this is being derived. There is just a person who has tried a lot of things and wants to give you the best of it for a price. That's the whole thing right now.
What's the vision?
Plato's Academy but with TRE and the slapping game. CFAR but the curriculum comes from noticing what actually moved something. An open-source monastery. A rationality dojo. Something like that.
Will I have to do a cold shower?
Yes. Cold showers are invaluable for noticing when you are contracting against something — including information — and learning to release. The showers are cold. This is intentional.
