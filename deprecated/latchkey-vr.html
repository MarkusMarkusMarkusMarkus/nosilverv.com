How it works
Three steps.
No scheduling.

Every home on Latchkey is scanned in full 3D. Browse, pick, and walk through — entirely on your own terms.

01

Browse listed homes

Search by area, price or size. Every property with a 🥽 badge has a full room-by-room 3D scan available — see exactly when it was captured.

02

Put on your headset

Compatible with Meta Quest, Apple Vision Pro, and most SteamVR devices. Open the tour link and you're standing in the hallway.

03

Walk every room

Shortlist from the couch, then only visit the ones that genuinely excite you. Or, if you've seen enough, go straight to the agent.

What you need
Two things
have to exist first.

VR tours are powerful — but they depend on both sides playing ball. Here's what makes it work.

🥽
For buyers

You need a VR headset

The tour is experienced through a VR device — that's what makes it feel like you're actually standing in the room, not watching a video.

  • Meta Quest 2, 3, or Pro
  • Apple Vision Pro
  • Any SteamVR-compatible headset
  • No headset? A 360° preview is available on any screen, though the immersion is reduced.
📡
For sellers

The home needs to be scanned

Before a VR tour can exist, the property has to be mapped — a professional scan using a Latchkey-certified capture device.

  • Takes about 45 minutes for a standard flat
  • Done by a Latchkey scan partner near you
  • Included free for all Latchkey listings
  • Re-scan available after major changes or staging
