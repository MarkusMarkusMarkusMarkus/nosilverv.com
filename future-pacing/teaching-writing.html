Writing

2027

Started teaching writing to Chris. Then word got around and people started sending me their stuff — essays, threads, posts, newsletters. I started editing. Not for grammar, for thought. Is this actually what you're trying to say? Is there a better shape for this?

It became something like an editorial practice for people writing for the internet. The format is loose — sometimes one session, sometimes ongoing. The question is always the same: what are you actually trying to communicate, and what's getting in the way.
