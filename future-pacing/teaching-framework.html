The Framework

2028

Five years of trying to articulate the same cluster of ideas — in tweets, in a glossary, in two books, in conversations that went late. At some point it became legible enough to take into rooms.

Conferences, workshops, the occasional invited talk. The format changed each time but the thing being communicated stayed the same. What it is, why it matters, what follows from it if you take it seriously.

The best talks were the ones where someone came up afterwards and said they'd been thinking about the same thing but couldn't name it. That's when you know the framework is doing its job.
